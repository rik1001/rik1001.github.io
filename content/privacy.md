# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: July 29, 2025*

This privacy policy outlines the data collection, processing, and protection practices employed by Astralis. Your privacy rights are treated with the same rigor and respect accorded to academic research standards.

## Information Collection Practices

### Automatically Collected Technical Data
Standard web server operations automatically collect certain information to ensure proper site functionality:

- **Technical Specifications**: Internet Protocol address, browser identification, device characteristics, operating system details
- **Usage Analytics**: Page access patterns, session duration, referral sources, navigation sequences
- **Performance Metrics**: Page load times, error occurrences, and technical performance data for optimization purposes

### Voluntarily Provided Information
- **Direct Communication**: Information shared through email correspondence or social media contact
- **Feedback and Comments**: Any voluntary communication or content submission

## Data Utilization Framework

Collected information serves exclusively to:

- **Enhance User Experience**: Analyzing visitor behavior patterns to optimize content presentation and site navigation
- **Technical Optimization**: Identifying and resolving technical issues to maintain optimal site performance
- **Content Strategy Development**: Understanding reader preferences to create more relevant and valuable content
- **Security Maintenance**: Protecting site integrity from spam, malicious activities, and security vulnerabilities

## Data Protection Principles

### What This Site Does Not Do

- **No Commercial Data Sales**: Personal information is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties for commercial purposes
- **No Invasive Tracking**: The site does not employ aggressive tracking mechanisms for advertising or marketing purposes
- **No Profile Construction**: Visitor data is not used to build detailed personal profiles for commercial exploitation
- **No Unsolicited Communication**: Contact information is never added to mailing lists or promotional campaigns without explicit consent

## Cookie and Tracking Technology

### Essential Functionality Cookies
Astralis employs minimal, necessary cookies for:
- Core site functionality and navigation
- User preference retention
- Basic analytical understanding of site usage patterns

### Third-Party Service Integration
This site may utilize:
- **GitHub Pages Hosting**: Subject to GitHub's Privacy Statement and data protection policies
- **Basic Analytics Services**: For understanding site usage patterns and performance optimization

## Data Security Measures

Information protection is maintained through:
- **Secure Hosting Infrastructure**: GitHub Pages provides encrypted, secure hosting environment
- **Minimal Data Collection**: Only necessary information for site operation is collected
- **Secure Communication Handling**: Personal communications are processed securely without unnecessary data retention

## Visitor Rights and Data Control

You maintain the following rights regarding your information:
- **Information Transparency**: Understanding what data is collected and how it is used
- **Data Access**: Requesting access to any personal information that may be stored
- **Data Correction**: Requesting correction of any inaccurate information
- **Data Deletion**: Requesting removal of personal information where legally permissible
- **Privacy Inquiry**: Contacting site administration regarding privacy concerns

## Privacy Philosophy and Commitment

Privacy protection represents a fundamental aspect of digital ethics and human dignity. This site exists to facilitate knowledge sharing and intellectual exchange, not to compromise visitor privacy or exploit personal information.

The commitment to privacy extends beyond legal compliance to encompass respect for individual autonomy and the principle that meaningful digital experiences require trust and transparency.
